# Chyako Cutz — Product Context

## Product Purpose
A mobile-first booking web app for Chyako Cutz, a barber shop in Sutton, London. Customers book haircut appointments; the owner manages the schedule via an owner dashboard.

## Register
product

## Users
- **Primary**: Local customers (men, teens to 40s) booking haircuts from their phones. Largely repeat visitors who know the shop.
- **Secondary**: Chyako (owner/master barber) managing daily bookings via owner dashboard.

## Brand
- Name: Chyako Cutz
- Tagline: Quality Hairstyles
- Location: Railway Bridge, High St, Sutton SM1 1JA
- Phone: +44 7888 658011
- Aesthetic: Bold, urban barbershop energy. Gold on near-black. All-caps typography. Razor-sharp, no fluff.

## Tone
Direct, confident, street-smart. Talks to customers like a barber would — "Pull up a chair", "Lock it in", "We got you." Never corporate, never soft.

## Anti-references
- Generic salon booking apps (Treatwell, Booksy) — too light, too corporate
- Material Design booking flows — too sterile
- Pastel / wellness aesthetics — completely wrong register

## Strategic principles
- Speed over ceremony: fewer taps, faster booking
- Confidence over hedging: never apologetic copy
- Mobile-first: designed for one thumb, standing outside the shop
